About Pi Classes

Where Students Become Board Toppers


Pi Classes is Perungudi’s leading coaching centre for Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics, located in Seevaram near CURI Hospital, Chennai. We combine deep conceptual understanding with board-smart exam techniques — the formula that consistently produces toppers.

Our experienced faculty delivers small-batch, personalised coaching to ensure every student gets the attention they deserve. Plus, we have a gaming zone to help students relax and recharge between sessions!

  • ▶Small batches — every student gets individual attention

  • ▶Proven track record producing school and district toppers

  • ▶Open 7 days a week — Mon–Fri 10AM–8PM, Sat–Sun 10AM–9PM
