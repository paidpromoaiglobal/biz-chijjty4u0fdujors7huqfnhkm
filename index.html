About Pi Classes

9 Years of Producing Board Toppers


Pi Classes is Perungudi’s leading coaching centre for Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics, located in Seevaram near CURI Hospital, Chennai. With 9 years of experience, we combine deep conceptual understanding with board-smart exam techniques — the formula that consistently produces toppers.

Our expert faculty delivers small-batch, personalised coaching for CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE and Matriculation boards, with weekly tests to track every student’s progress. Plus, a gaming zone to help students recharge between sessions!
